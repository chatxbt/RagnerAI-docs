## First Steps

- **Request Access:** Begin by requesting access to our platform through [this form](https://ragner.ai/request-access).
- **Onboarding:** Our team will reach out to schedule a meeting to discuss your specific needs. Please monitor your email for our correspondence.
- **Credentials:** After completing the initial onboarding steps, we will provide you with both staging and production credentials.
- **Development:** We recommend developing and testing in the staging environment before deploying to production.
